About ICT Student Hub

ICT Student Hub in an informative and accesible portal of all relevant knowledge required by the ICT students of Bangladesh University of Professionals. It is enriched with knowledge about different academic courses, the in-house faculties in charge, the bus route to attend University, the booklist of desired courses and information regarding internship to enrich the student. ICT Student Hub is nothing but the mesh of knoclwedge working as a guidline to our fellow ICT students.
